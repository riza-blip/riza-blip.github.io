Windows

Windows is a series of operating systems developed by Microsoft. It is known for its user-friendly interface and compatibility with a wide range of software applications.

Mac OS

Mac OS, developed by Apple Inc., is known for its sleek interface and integration with Apple’s ecosystem of devices. It is popular among creative professionals for its robust performance and stability.

Linux

Linux is an open-source operating system that is known for its flexibility and security. It is widely used in server environments and is favored by developers and tech enthusiasts for its customization options.

iOS

iOS is Apple's mobile operating system, designed exclusively for iPhone and iPad devices. It is known for its smooth performance, intuitive user interface, and tight integration with the Apple ecosystem.

Android

Android, developed by Google, is a versatile operating system used in a variety of devices, including smartphones and tablets. It offers a customizable user experience and a large selection of applications through the Google Play Store.
