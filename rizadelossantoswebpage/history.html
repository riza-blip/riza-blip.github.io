Baga Burger History

Baga Burger, the first and only Buy 1, Take 1 grilled burger in the Philippines, was founded in Baliuag, Bulacan. Known for its delicious, flame-grilled patties, Baga Burger quickly gained popularity for its unique offering of grilled burgers at a great value. With its tasty and affordable menu, it has expanded beyond Baliuag and now boasts more than 10 franchise branches across the country. Baga Burger has become a beloved local favorite, offering Filipinos an irresistible combination of quality and value in every bite.
